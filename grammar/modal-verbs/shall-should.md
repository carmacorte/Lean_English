# Shall / Should

## Purpose

**Shall** and **should** are modal verbs related to suggestions, offers, advice, and recommendations.

In modern English, **should** is much more common than **shall**. **Shall** is mostly used in questions, offers, formal language, or British English.

## 1. Shall

**Shall** is used to offer help, make suggestions, or ask for instructions. It is usually used with **I** or **we**.

### Structure

```text
Shall + subject + base verb?
```

### Examples

| English | Spanish |
|---|---|
| Shall we go to the park? | ¿Vamos al parque? |
| Shall I open the window? | ¿Abro la ventana? |
| Shall we start the meeting? | ¿Empezamos la junta? |

### Common Uses

| Use | Example | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Suggestion | Shall we review the report? | ¿Revisamos el reporte? |
| Offer | Shall I help you? | ¿Te ayudo? |
| Formal instruction | The supplier shall provide evidence. | El proveedor deberá proporcionar evidencia. |

## 2. Should

**Should** is used to give advice, make recommendations, or express what is expected.

### Structure

```text
Subject + should + base verb
```

### Examples

| English | Spanish |
|---|---|
| You should eat more vegetables. | Deberías comer más verduras. |
| He should study more. | Él debería estudiar más. |
| We should review the data before the meeting. | Deberíamos revisar los datos antes de la junta. |

## Shall vs Should

| Situation | Preferred Option | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Giving advice | Should | You should practice every day. |
| Making a suggestion | Shall | Shall we continue? |
| Offering help | Shall | Shall I explain it again? |
| Expressing expectation | Should | The process should be stable. |
| Formal requirement | Shall | The supplier shall submit the report. |

## Common Mistakes

| Incorrect | Correct | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| You should to study. | You should study. | Do not use **to** after should. |
| She should studies. | She should study. | Use the base verb after should. |
| Shall you help me? | Will you help me? / Can you help me? | **Shall** is normally used with I/we. |

## Manufacturing English Examples

| English | Spanish |
|---|---|
| Quality should review the control plan. | Calidad debería revisar el plan de control. |
| We should validate the first article inspection. | Deberíamos validar la inspección de primer artículo. |
| Shall we review the defect trend? | ¿Revisamos la tendencia de defectos? |
| The supplier shall provide dimensional evidence. | El proveedor deberá proporcionar evidencia dimensional. |
