# Must / Have To

## Purpose

**Must** and **have to** are used to express obligation or necessity. Both can be translated as **deber** or **tener que**, but they are not always used in the same way.

## 1. Must

**Must** is used for strong obligation, rules, or logical deduction.

### Structure

```text
Subject + must + base verb
```

### Examples

| English | Spanish |
|---|---|
| You must wear a seatbelt. | Debes usar cinturón de seguridad. |
| You must follow the safety rules. | Debes seguir las reglas de seguridad. |
| It must be very cold outside. | Debe hacer mucho frío afuera. |

### Common Uses

| Use | Example | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Strong obligation | You must wear safety glasses. | Es obligatorio. |
| Logical deduction | He is not answering. He must be busy. | Probablemente está ocupado. |

## 2. Have To

**Have to** also expresses obligation, but it usually refers to external rules, duties, schedules, or responsibilities.

### Structure

```text
Subject + have/has to + base verb
```

### Examples

| English | Spanish |
|---|---|
| I have to finish my homework. | Tengo que terminar mi tarea. |
| She has to work tomorrow. | Ella tiene que trabajar mañana. |
| We have to submit the report today. | Tenemos que entregar el reporte hoy. |

## Must vs Have To

| Situation | Preferred Option | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Personal or strong obligation | Must | I must improve my English. |
| External rule or requirement | Have to | I have to wear a badge at work. |
| Safety rule | Must / Have to | You must wear safety glasses. |
| Daily responsibility | Have to | I have to attend the meeting. |

## Common Mistakes

| Incorrect | Correct | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| She musts study. | She must study. | After a modal verb, use the base verb. |
| I have finish this. | I have to finish this. | Use **have to**, not only **have**. |
| He have to work. | He has to work. | Use **has to** with he/she/it. |

## Manufacturing English Examples

| English | Spanish |
|---|---|
| Operators must follow the work instruction. | Los operadores deben seguir el instructivo de trabajo. |
| Quality has to verify the defect evidence. | Calidad tiene que verificar la evidencia del defecto. |
| The unit must pass functional test before packing. | La unidad debe pasar prueba funcional antes de empaque. |
| We have to review the traceability record. | Tenemos que revisar el registro de trazabilidad. |
